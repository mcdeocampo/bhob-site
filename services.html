Public Services

Public Services

View common public services, requirements, processing times, fees, and responsible offices.

Official certification for legal, employment, business, or personal purposes.

Requirements: Valid ID, proof of residence, accomplished request form Processing Time: Same day to 1 working day Fees: Based on approved barangay schedule Office: Barangay Secretary

Proof that the applicant is a resident of Barangay Hulo.

Requirements: Valid ID and proof of address Processing Time: Same day Fees: May be free or based on ordinance Office: Barangay Secretary

Certification for residents requesting assistance or benefits.

Requirements: Valid ID, interview/verification, proof of need Processing Time: 1 working day Fees: Usually free Office: Punong Barangay / Barangay Secretary

Barangay endorsement for business permit processing.

Requirements: Business details, valid ID, lease/proof of location Processing Time: 1-2 working days Fees: Based on ordinance Office: Barangay Treasurer

Certificate for eligible first-time jobseekers.

Requirements: Valid ID, oath of undertaking, residency proof Processing Time: Same day to 1 working day Fees: Free for qualified applicants Office: Barangay Secretary

Recording and assistance for complaints, incidents, and mediation.

Requirements: Valid ID and incident details Processing Time: Depends on case Fees: Free Office: Barangay Desk Officer

Basic health support and referral assistance.

Requirements: Valid ID, health concern details Processing Time: As scheduled Fees: Free Office: Barangay Health Workers

Support and coordination for senior citizen concerns.

Requirements: Senior citizen ID and request details Processing Time: As scheduled Fees: Free Office: Senior Citizen Desk

Assistance and coordination for persons with disabilities.

Requirements: PWD ID or supporting documents Processing Time: As scheduled Fees: Free Office: PWD Desk

Urgent support during emergencies, floods, fires, and disasters.

Requirements: Incident details and location Processing Time: Immediate response Fees: Free Office: BDRRMC / Tanods

Youth activities, sports, leadership, and development programs.

Requirements: Age/residency details when required Processing Time: As scheduled Fees: Free Office: SK Chairperson
