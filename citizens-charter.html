Service Standards

Service Standards

Clear guide to frontline service standards, requirements, processing steps, processing time, fees, and responsible offices.

Service Pledge

Barangay Hulo commits to provide courteous, timely, transparent, and accessible service to all residents and stakeholders.

Step Process Processing Time Responsible Office
1 Submit accomplished form and requirements 5 minutes Receiving Desk
2 Verification of records and residency 10-20 minutes Barangay Secretary
3 Assessment of applicable fees 5 minutes Barangay Treasurer
4 Approval and signature 10-30 minutes Punong Barangay / Authorized Official
5 Release of document 5 minutes Releasing Desk

Requirements

Valid ID, proof of residence, completed request form, and other documents depending on the requested service.

Feedback and Complaint Process

Residents may submit feedback at the Barangay Hall, through the contact form, or via the official Facebook page. Complaints will be logged and referred to the responsible office.
